Our Mission



Of all the people who have ever lived, only a handful have been blessed with the experience of walking across the moon. We had a vision of a world where the rest of humanity could one day touch the stars! After years of study and experimentation, our team has finally devised and built the first ships capable of rapid traversal across the solar system! Before, it would take at least a few years to reach Saturn, but with our new ships, that journey would only take a day! It is our great honor to finally give humanity the means to travel across the solar system not just for the sake of exploration, but also as a vacation! Don't worry, our company has already travelled to the available destinations and built luxurious and habitable resorts designed to withstand the harsh extraterrestrial environments! We hope you take this oppurtunity to go where no man could previously go before!
